Inspiration

Logos

The SpeakEZ logo is inspired by the seamless innovation of the Vision Pro experience. The clean, futuristic design reflects the app’s advanced technology, mirroring how the Vision Pro immerses users in an innovative, distraction-free environment. The bold "Speak" conveys the confidence and assertiveness that the app helps users develop, while the "EZ" encased in a speech bubble speaks to the app’s ease of use—delivering feedback and guidance in a way that feels natural and intuitive. The color scheme, with calming blues and a dark backdrop, captures the immersive, tech-forward feel of the Vision Pro, allowing users to focus on refining their public speaking skills in a virtual space designed for modern, effortless improvement.

Colors

The inspiration for this color palette draws from the immersive experience of using the Vision Pro, where sleek, futuristic tones set the stage for innovation. "Steady Focus" (#242424) mimics the dark, unobtrusive backgrounds often seen in virtual spaces, allowing the user to concentrate on their speech without distraction—just like standing in the shadows before taking the stage. "Blue Spotlight" (#5382dd) reflects the bright, dynamic hues seen when engaging with augmented elements, symbolizing the spotlight's energy as the user practices engaging an audience. "Feedback Gray" (#d1d2d8), a calming, light tone, echoes the neutral, sophisticated feedback environment, where clarity and improvement come to life. Together, these colors evoke a sense of confidence, clarity, and progress, supporting users as they prepare to deliver standout presentations.

Fonts

The inspiration behind the font choices for SpeakEZ stems from the need to balance boldness with readability, reflecting both confidence and accessibility. Montserrat Black 900 is used for headings to create a commanding presence, much like a speaker on stage, immediately capturing attention with its strong, modern lines. This font choice conveys authority and professionalism, key qualities in public speaking. For the body text, Montserrat Regular Medium ensures a clean and approachable reading experience, maintaining clarity and ease of navigation as users interact with the app. Both fonts complement the sleek, innovative design of the Vision Pro, aligning with the app’s mission of making public speaking both powerful and effortless.
